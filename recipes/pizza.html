Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

Description

This recipe will show you that even a regular joe can make an oustanding pizza! Making it homemade is the route to go.
Using basic ingredients from the store. it will be great, fast, and easy!

Ingredients for our Homemade Pepperoni Pizza

  • one store-bought pizza crust
  • two bags of shredded mozzarella
  • two jars of pizza sauce
  • one big bag of pepperonis

With these ingredients ready, we can begin with our recipe.

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 400 degrees
  2. Unwrap pizza crust
  3. Open your sauce and spread it on your pizza
  4. Open your mozarella and add as much cheese as you'd like
  5. Place all of your pepperonis
  6. Place in the oven and bake for 20-23 minutes
  7. Take your pizza out when it's golden brown on the crust, cut, and enjoy!
