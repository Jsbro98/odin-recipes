Spaghetti and Meatballs

Description

A household classic that will sure to satisfy.

Ingredients for our Spaghetti and Meatballs

  • One bag of Spaghetti pasta
  • One bag of frozen meatballs
  • A jar of pasta sauce

With our ingredients ready, we can begin with our recipe

  1. Fill a pot with water, set to boil
  2. After boiling, open your pasta and put it in your pot
  3. Cook your pasta until it is soft (you can taste a peice for consistency)
  4. Strain your pasta
  5. Place your pasta back in the pot and add your jar of sauce, lower your burner
  6. Open and cook your meatballs as directed in the microwave or oven
  7. Place your meatballs in the pasta and stir
  8. cook it all together on low for 10-15 minutes
  9. serve and enjoy!
