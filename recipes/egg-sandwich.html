Egg Sandwich

Description

A simple TikTok trend that revolutionized egg sandwiches for simplicity!

Ingredients for our Egg Sandwich

  • 2-4 eggs
  • 2 pieces of bread

    • Optional Ingredients:

  • 1 peice of american cheese
  • 1 bag of bacon bits

With our ingredients, we can begin our recipe

  1. Turn your burner to medium
  2. Crack eggs into a cup, whisk
  3. Pour eggs into your pan
  4. Place your bread in the pan on opposite sides, mirrored from each other, soak both sides in the egg mixture
  5. Flip the sides of the eggs inn towards the middle of the sandwich
  6. Add your cheese and bacon bits on top of one of the peices of bread
  7. Fold your sandwich in on itself
  8. Serve and enjoy!
